About

Using theatre as a tool to connect people is the core of my creative practice. I believe honouring authentic voice allows underrepresented stories to be heard.

Edinburgh-based, working across Scotland; originally from Dundee and willing to travel.

Timeline

Key milestones.

SEPT 2025

DEWAR ARTS AWARD RECIPIENT

In order to complete her second year at Edinburgh Napier University on the MFA Directing course, Meghan received the Dewar Arts Award for exceptional young artists.

AUG 2025

EDINBURGH FRINGE

Meghan was assistant director to Ian Dun on You Have Failed Me For The Last Time by Lucas McQueen with New Celts Productions. She also produced Derby Day and Wish You Were Here for Without Compromise Theatre.

OCT 2024

NEW CELTS PRODUCTIONS

Meghan was assistant director to Iain Davie on his production of Europe by David Greig with New Celts Productions.

JAN 2024

WITHOUT COMPROMISE THEATRE

Meghan, Michael and Lucy founded Without Compromise Theatre for a run of the Edinburgh Fringe. We received 5-star reviews and toured Wish You Were Here nationally.

OCT 2023

GATEWAY NEW WRITING FESTIVAL

Meghan directed the world premiere of Instead of Pines by Amy Davidson. a piece of sci-fi theatre which was performed in the Scottish Storytelling Centre.

AUG 2022

EDINBURGH FRINGE

Meghan was co-director for the world premiere of Cicely and David by David Clark, a piece which explored the life and work of Cicely Saunders. The film of this production has since toured internationally with the piece being captioned and translated in multiple languages.
